MISSION

At the Transformers Project, we strive to ensure that young womyn feel empowered and confident in Robotics and STEM fields.



Lady Bots Are...

Brilliant, strong and resilient young women of color who aren't afraid of beating the odds in the face of adversity and succeeding.

NYC Teens from under-resourced communities between the ages of 12-16 who have a passion and/or interest in robotics, coding and STEM.
