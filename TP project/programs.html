Transformers Lead!

Our Transformers Lead program will focus on after school enrichment. Ambassadors work with students at our partner schools and organizations to introduce basic robot building and programming techniques that will culminate semesterly demonstration for family and friends.



Ready...Set...Build!

Ready...Set... Build! is all about robot construction! Students will compete individually to build the most unique robot and tie it's use to the theme of the semester. This year's theme is SOCIAL JUSTICE!



Lady Bots League

The Lady Bots are our organizations competing robotics team! They compete in tounaments all over New York City, as well NY State. To be eligible for Lady Bots League, students must have at least two years of involvement and 85% attendance at the Transformers Project.
